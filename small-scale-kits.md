# Building Whistle Kits: Small Scale (Up to ~1,000 units)

For quantities up to approximately 1,000 kits, purchasing pre-made parts and assembling them locally is the most economical approach.

## What You'll Need

### Materials
- **Whistles** - Buy in bulk from online retailers (Amazon, eBay, AliExpress, etc.)
- **Lanyards** - Available from outdoor/craft suppliers or online
- **Tarjeta de Derechos (Rights Card)** - Print from the provided [Español version](redcard_es.pdf)
- **"Form a Crowd, Stay Loud" Zine** - Order printed copies from a local print shop
- **Small Baggies** - 4x6" or 5x8" ziplock bags from office/bulk suppliers
- **Optional: Labels or stickers** - For marking kits with your location/contact info

### Tools & Equipment
- A table or workspace for assembly
- Community volunteers for assembly line
- Packaging supplies (tape if needed)

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1. **Source Your Materials**

**Whistles** (cost: ~$0.60 per unit in quantities of 1,000 on Amazon)
- Search "bulk whistles" on Amazon for reliable sourcing
- Order in quantities of 1,000+ for best pricing
- Quick shipping from Amazon (Prime-eligible)
- Test a sample before committing to large order

**Lanyards** (cost: ~$0.10 per unit in quantities of 1,000 on Amazon)
- Search "lanyard" on Amazon
- Look for durable, adjustable lanyards that fit comfortably
- Order in same quantity as whistles
- Quick shipping available

**Zines** (cost: $0.15-0.50 per unit depending on print shop and order quantity)
- The "Form a Crowd, Stay Loud" zine is an 8-page journal fold - a single sheet printed and folded
- Get quotes from local print shops (usually cheaper than national chains)
- Larger print runs (500+) get better per-unit pricing
- Order flat, unfolded—recipients will fold as part of kit assembly
- Typical turnaround: 1-2 weeks

**Rights Cards (Tarjeta de Derechos)** (cost: $0.05-0.15 per unit)
- Print locally on cardstock
- Can be printed at office supply stores or local printers in bulk
- Print on both sides to get 8 cards per page, or single-sided for 4 cards per page
- Package uncut—recipients will cut and separate as needed when assembling kits

### 2. **Prepare Materials**

**Inspect whistles**
- Check for defects, cracks, or manufacturing issues
- Test sound quality on a sample
- Set aside any defective units

### 3. **Packing Materials into Kits**

Your "kit" is a bundle of supplies that recipients will assemble into whistle baggies. Each kit contains:

1. **Unfolded zines** - Recipients fold as part of baggie assembly
2. **Uncut rights cards** (full pages) - Recipients will cut and separate as needed
3. **Whistles** (attached to lanyards)
4. **Baggies** - Empty 4x6" bags for recipients to fill with assembled whistle baggies
5. **Instructions** - Include simple illustrated instructions for folding zines, cutting cards, assembling, and sealing baggies

Pack all materials into a larger kit box/package with clear labeling.

### 4. **Quality Check & Packaging**

- Attach whistles to lanyards before packing in kits
- Use a checklist for each kit: ☐ Whistles+Lanyards ☐ Unfolded Zines ☐ Uncut Cards ☐ Baggies ☐ Instructions
- Include clear, illustrated assembly instructions with each kit
- Pack complete kits in boxes for easy distribution
- Consider adding a label with your organization's name, location, and contact info

### 5. **Distribution - Getting Whistles WORN and VISIBLE**

The goal is not just to make whistles, but to get them **worn visibly** in your community. Visible whistles on lanyards create a powerful signal of collective protection.

**Local distribution:**
- Distribute kit bundles to community organizations, mutual aid groups, and volunteers who will actively promote wearing whistles
- Recipients use the materials to assemble baggies for their neighbors
- Encourage wearers to showcase their whistles - the visibility is powerful
- Partner with local organizations to coordinate assembly activities and ensure whistles end up being worn
- Set up kit distribution at protests, community events, or markets where people will take them home and wear them

**Shipping:**
- **FedEx OneRate is the lowest cost and most reliable option**
- A cube-shaped FedEx Large Box fits a complete kit (360 whistles) perfectly
- Ships 2nd day for approximately $30 per box
- Use OneRate pricing for the best rates
- Label boxes clearly with contents and assembly instructions

## Timeline

- Week 1: Finalize design/zine content, get print quotes
- Week 2-3: Order whistles, lanyards, print zines
- Week 3-4: Print/cut rights cards, prepare workspace
- Week 4: Assembly party (1-2 days depending on quantity)
- Week 4+: Distribute or ship kits

## Tips for Success

- **Start small**: Prepare 10 kits first to test the process
- **Clear instructions**: Include illustrated assembly instructions showing how to fold zines, cut cards, assemble baggies, and seal
- **Quality check**: Make sure each kit has all components
- **Distribute strategically**: Give kits to volunteers and organizations who will actually assemble and distribute whistle baggies
- **Document**: Take photos of kit assembly and distribution—great for social media
- **Follow up**: Check in with recipients to see how many whistle baggies they assembled and distributed
